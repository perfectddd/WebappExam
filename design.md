Design System: Royal Discipline
1. Brand Essence
Core Theme: Minimal Modern Military
Target Audience: Thai Government & Armed Forces
Visual Identity: Authoritative, disciplined, clean, and professional.
2. Design Tokens
Colors
Primary: #2E4632 (Deep Forest Green) - Used for primary actions, branding, and status indicators.
Surface: #F7FAFC (Cool Gray White) - Main background for screens.
Surface-Dim: #D7DADC - Used for secondary backgrounds and subtle borders.
Surface-Container: #FFFFFF - Card backgrounds and elevated elements.
Success: (Inferred) Dark Green for "Passed" status.
Outline: #E2E8F0 - Soft borders for cards and inputs.
Typography
Primary Font: Be Vietnam Pro
Headlines: Bold, high contrast, using #2E4632.
Body: Regular/Medium weight, high legibility for long-form reading.
Spacing & Shape
Roundness: ROUND_EIGHT (8px border radius) for a modern yet stable feel.
Layout: Generous whitespace to maintain clarity in high-pressure environments (exams).
3. UI Components Patterns
Cards: Elevated with soft shadows or subtle borders (border-outline-variant).
Buttons: Solid primary green for main actions, outlined or ghost for secondary actions.
Progress Indicators: Circular progress for results; linear progress bars for active exams.
Status Badges: Rounded pills with high-contrast text to signal exam status.
